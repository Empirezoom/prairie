# PrairieWealth Luxury Redesign: Visual Concepts

I have developed two distinct architectural directions for the PrairieWealth overhaul. Since our image generation capacity is currently on cooldown, I have provided high-precision **Style Guides** that define the exact visual DNA for each option. 

Each option is designed to meet the "Senior Developer" standard of excellence—focusing on depth, hierarchy, and tactile premium feel.

---

## Option 1: The "Midnight & Gold" (Royal Heritage)
This design focuses on historical prestige, trust, and old-wealth aesthetics. It uses a deep indigo-black base with burnished gold accents.

### Visual DNA
- **Base Background**: `Midnight Indigo (#050b18)` — A dark, saturated navy that feels heavier and more expensive than pure black.
- **Primary Accent**: `Burnished Gold (#d4af37)` — Used for key CTAs and borders, with a subtle 45-degree linear gradient to `Sand Gold (#f9e5af)`.
- **Surface Layer**: `Glassmorphic Pearl (#ffffff0a)` — Cards use 10% white opacity with `backdrop-filter: blur(40px)`.
- **Typography**: 
    - **Header**: *Outfit* (Black weight, 700-900). Spaced at `-0.05em`.
    - **Subheader**: *Inter* (Medium weight). Spaced at `0.2em` for that editorial luxury look.

### Interface Details (Desktop & Mobile)
- **The Metal Card**: Account balances are presented on a virtual "Metal Card" with a brushed texture and 3D depth shadows.
- **Haptic Navigation**: On mobile, a floating bottom navigation bar in gold-tinted glass.
- **Audit Ledger**: Transactions appear as "Engraved" entries on the glass surface.

---

## Option 2: The "Obsidian & Platinum" (Cyber-Wealth)
A more tech-forward, futuristic, and ultra-minimalist approach. Inspired by dark-mode trading terminals and high-end automotive interfaces.

### Visual DNA
- **Base Background**: `Pure Obsidian (#000000)` — Total darkness for maximum OLED contrast.
- **Primary Accent**: `Cyber Platinum (#e2e2e2)` — A cold, metallic silver that glows when active.
- **Surface Layer**: `Shadow Glass (#0a0a0a80)` — Darker glass with high-intensity `box-shadow` instead of borders.
- **Typography**:
    - **Header**: *Syne* (Bold) or *Geist Mono* for a professional, encrypted-ledger feel.
    - **Data**: All numbers use a high-contrast sans-serif for absolute clarity.

### Interface Details (Desktop & Mobile)
- **Command Center**: The dashboard is treated as a "Mission Control" center with data-heavy charts and minimalist silver strokes.
- **Biometric Identity**: The "Login" and "Verify" pages feature a glowing platinum ring that pulses like a biometric scanner.
- **Liquid Data**: Hover effects use "mercury" style liquid transitions.
